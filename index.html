Frequently Asked Questions

What is the purpose of HDPE pipes in infrastructure? ▼

HDPE pipes are designed to handle high-pressure water and gas distribution with extreme durability, offering 50+ years of service life and leak-proof joints.

What are the benefits of using HDPE over traditional materials? ▼

HDPE is corrosion-resistant, flexible, and has superior impact strength compared to PVC or ductile iron pipes.

How are these pipes joined during installation? ▼

They are typically joined using butt fusion or electrofusion, creating a joint that is as strong as or stronger than the pipe itself.
